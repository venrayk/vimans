ABOUT US 关于我们

VIMANS 唯曼丝袜业

Chengdu Weitzmann’s Cloth Co. Ltd was established at the year of 2016 in Chengdu, the beautiful city which is praised as the “the land of abundance”. Upholding the concepts of Integrity, Innovation, Elegance, Comfortable, Expertise and Simple, our company adhere to offer good quality of service to all the businessmen and high-end people. The system of total quality control has been applied by professionals through the whole process, including selecting cotton, spinning, dyeing, weaving and fabric setting. We take pride in our product for passing the test of ISO9001-2015 International Quality System and getting National Standard Examination Research Institution’s Certification.

Depending on advanced and scientific technology advantages, our company believes that, the combing cotton mixed with 60% of 131A and 40% of 137A, originating from Akesu, Xinjiang, through advanced vortex spinning and DuPont’s wrapping of Lycra, can create the unique boutique which cosseted every guest in the most environment-friendly, original and comfortable way. “Born nobly, and Born for you.”is our central tenet in producing. Your satisfaction is our motivation.

Your journey begins at your feet. Weitzmann’s will accompany you on every step.
